What is SportsSim?

Articles | Written by: Samuel Gibson, Justin Kelly



     SportsSim is the creation of software developers Justin Kelly and Samuel Gibson. The birth of the project is the culmination of several factors. Our primary drive for creating this website was our passion for sports, specifically, our interest in the National Basketball Association. The idea for this project originally started back in the Fall of 2019, when we created a prototype of the website for our senior capstone project. Our goal was to create a website that would display player stats in an accessible and clear way. We are huge fans of the game of basketball, and felt that many of the current website interfaces aren't very user friendly. We set out to create a stripped back user interface that even individuals who are just jumping into the game of basketball could understand.


     The original name of the project was called NBASTAT. Our capstone project focused primarily on a creating a *relatively* sleek design. This project was our first real dive into front end development and design. We wanted to structure the huge amount player data in a way that was easily digestable. The ultimate goal with our capstone was to make our front page and player pages sleek enough that we wouldn't have to make any of the other pages that you would expect with a sports data website. After finishing our capstone class, the NBASTAT project was dropped, now that it's development wasn't affecting our grade we had little reason to continue the project.
